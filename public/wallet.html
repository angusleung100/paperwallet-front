Instructions

  1. Print off this page (Ctrl + P). The paper wallet front and back are set to the same size as a standard credit card.
  2. Cut along the dotted borders around the front and back portions of the paper wallet card.
  3. Cut a 3.37" by 2.125" rectangular piece of thin carton (Granola bar boxes are perfect).
  4. Glue both portions of the paper wallet card to the front and back sides of the thin carton. Carefully align the edges.
  5. Enjoy your new paper wallet! :)
